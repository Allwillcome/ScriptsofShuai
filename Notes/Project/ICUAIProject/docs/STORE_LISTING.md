## Detailed Description

### English Version

**Transform Your Training Data into Actionable Insights**

Intervals.icu AI Coach brings the power of artificial intelligence directly to your training dashboard. Get instant, personalized analysis of your workouts, sleep data, and recovery metrics - all with a single click.

**🎯 Key Features:**

• **Intelligent Analysis** - Advanced AI models analyze your heart rate zones, pace, duration, training load, and recovery patterns to provide expert-level insights

• **13+ AI Services Supported** - Choose from OpenAI (ChatGPT), Anthropic (Claude), DeepSeek, Google Gemini, and 10 more providers. Use the AI service you trust most.

• **Privacy First** - Your data stays between you, Intervals.icu, and your chosen AI service. We don't collect, store, or track anything.

• **Seamless Integration** - Embedded floating widget appears right on your Intervals.icu activity pages. No need to copy-paste or switch tabs.

• **Context-Aware Coaching** - Get advice tailored to YOUR training patterns and current fitness state, not generic recommendations.

• **Multi-Language Support** - Receive analysis in your preferred language.

**📊 What You Get:**

Every analysis includes:
- Overall workout impression with specific metrics
- 2-4 actionable observations combining data and recommendations
- Friendly, coach-like tone that's easy to understand
- Specific suggestions for your next training session

**⚙️ Easy Setup:**

1. Install the extension
2. Add your AI service API key (one-time setup)
3. Visit any Intervals.icu activity page
4. Click "Analyze" and get instant insights

**🔒 Your Privacy Matters:**

- No data collection or tracking
- All settings stored locally on your device
- Direct API calls to your chosen AI service
- You control everything with your own API keys

**Perfect for:**
- Endurance athletes (runners, cyclists, triathletes)
- Coaches analyzing athlete data
- Anyone using Intervals.icu for training planning

**Get Started Today:**

Turn your raw training data into wisdom. Make every workout count with AI-powered insights from Intervals.icu AI Coach.
